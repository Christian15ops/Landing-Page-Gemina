Our Mission & Vision

MSU-MCEST is a campus of Mindanao State University committed to providing quality education in the fields of Education, Science, and Technology. As a state-supported institution, we serve the communities of Northern Mindanao by producing competent, ethical, and service-oriented graduates.

We aim to be a center of excellence in instruction, research, extension, and production that contributes to the social, economic, and cultural development of the region.
