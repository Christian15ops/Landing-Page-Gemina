About MSU-MCEST

MSU-MCEST (Mindanao State University – Maigo College of Education Science and Technology) is a constituent college of the MSU System, located in Maigo, Lanao del Norte. Established in 2001, it has grown into a recognized center of learning in Northern Mindanao.

The college is committed to producing competent, ethical, and service-oriented graduates through quality instruction, relevant research, and meaningful community extension services. It celebrates its Silver Anniversary as a milestone of two decades of academic excellence.
